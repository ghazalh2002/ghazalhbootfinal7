About Ghazaleh

A story of passion, tradition, and culinary excellence

Named after the Arbic word for "gazelle" - symbolizing grace, beauty, and elegance - Ghazaleh Restaurant is a space where elegance meets warmth — a place inspired by nature, seasons, and the quiet beauty of light in darkness.

Our story began with a simple yet meaningful vision: to craft dishes that awaken the senses and connect people through flavor, care, and creativity. Every recipe is prepared with intention, using fresh ingredients and time-honored methods.

From golden saffron touches to delicate herbal infusions, every item on our menu is designed to evoke feeling — not just taste. Here, food is an experience: a moment of reflection, beauty, and belonging.
